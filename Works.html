Literary Works of José Rizal

The Philippines a century hence

by José Rizal
José Rizal, in this short work (which originally appeared in Spanish in the Filipino newspaper La Solidaridad, September 1889-January 1890) gives a prediction of the future of the Philippines.
Publish Date: 1912
Language: English
Pages: 136

Other Literary Works

Noli Me Tangere (1887)

A powerful novel exposing the abuses and injustices of Spanish colonial rule in the Philippines.

El Filibusterismo (1891)

The sequel to Noli Me Tangere, presenting a darker approach to social reform.

Mi Último Adiós (1896)

A farewell poem written on the eve of his execution.
